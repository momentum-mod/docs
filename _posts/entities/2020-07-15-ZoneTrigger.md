## Keyvalues
>**Zone Number** (zone_number&lt;**integer**&gt;)

The zone number of this trigger indicates:
 - **0**: Invalid/Stop trigger
 - **1**: Start trigger
 - **>1**: Stage/Checkpoint trigger
