title: mom_stage_tele
category: command
tags:

required_params: 
  - Stage number
optional_params:
  - Track number
---

Teleports the player to the desired stage in a track. Also stops the timer.

## Usage Examples

> `mom_stage_tele 3`

Teleports the player to stage 3 in their current track.

> `mom_stage_tele 3 2`

Teleports the player to stage 3 in track 2.
